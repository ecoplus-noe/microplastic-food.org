Our mission:

Development of a valid method to detect different types of microplastics in liquid foods.

"microplastic@food" will use a new AI-based algorithm to determine the type of microplastic particles and their source of origin in selected beverages and foods. Based on an analytical validation, we aim to identify possible microplastic sources such as filling, packaging or environment. Our research results will directly feed into a handbook that will be made available to key stakeholders and corporate partners in packaging and food production in order to avoid in-house management strategies for microplastic contamination in food.
